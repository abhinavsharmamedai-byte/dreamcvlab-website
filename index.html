Principal Investigator
Abhinav Sharma, MD, PhD

Associate Professor, Division of Cardiology, McGill University. Scientist and Director, DREAM-CV Lab, Research Institute of the MUHC.

Dr. Sharma is a heart failure cardiologist and clinician-scientist whose research program bridges cardiometabolic clinical trials, digital health innovation, and precision phenotyping to improve outcomes for patients with heart failure and type 2 diabetes.

He directs the DECIDE-CV Cardiometabolic Program at the MUHC, serves as MD/PhD Program Director at McGill University, and chairs the Research Committee of the Canadian Heart Failure Society.

Trained at McMaster (MD), Alberta (PhD, Cardiology), Duke (Research Fellow), and Stanford (Advanced HF & Transplant).

What We Do
Research Pillars

Our work spans three interconnected themes aimed at transforming cardiovascular-metabolic care.

Cardiometabolic Trials & Outcomes

Investigating SGLT2 inhibitors, GLP-1 receptor agonists, and novel therapies across heart failure, diabetes, and kidney disease. Leading the DECIDE-CV program — a synchronous care model integrating cardiology, endocrinology, and nephrology. Key trial publications: CANVAS, CREDENCE, EMPA-REG, EMPEROR, EXAMINE.

Digital Health & AI

Pioneering voice-based AI screening (VOICE-COVID trials), wearable biosensors for heart failure detection (DEPLOY-HF, WEAR-HF, VALIDATE-HF), and the application of large language models in cardiology. Published in Nature Medicine on physicians as "context engineers" in the AI era.

Precision Phenotyping

Using latent class analysis, multi-omics profiling, and clinical clustering to move beyond one-size-fits-all approaches. The DECIDEOMIC program combines clinical data with metabolomics and proteomics to identify actionable cardiovascular phenotypes in type 2 diabetes.
