The FOOD-1 Paradox

In the trial, 51% of participants were "dual responders" whose LDL and TMAO both improved on the plant diet. Paradoxically, these same responders showed the largest NT-proBNP increase +0.35 log units, p=0.005 vs non-responders +0.02, p=0.83.

This likely reflects transient sodium/volume effects from processed plant products, not cardiac harm. Adherence was identical between groups (5% deviation rate in both).
